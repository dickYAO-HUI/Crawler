A Brinks armored car lies on its side. A strongbox spills $100 bills out into the street. They flutter idly In the wind.

EXT. BROOKLYN BRIDGE - DUSK

The R.V. races over the deserted bridge, headed for Manhattan.

THE CAMERA CRANES UP to reveal the New York skyline. Both familiar, yet terrifyingly different. For now there is no movement, no lights, no sign of life.

The sun sinks beneath the tall buildings, and the tiny R.V. is swallowed by the silent city.

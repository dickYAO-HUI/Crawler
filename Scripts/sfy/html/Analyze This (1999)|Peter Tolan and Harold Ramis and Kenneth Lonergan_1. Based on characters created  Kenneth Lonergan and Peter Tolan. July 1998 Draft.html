Analyze This (1999)

by Peter Tolan and Harold Ramis and Kenneth Lonergan.
Based on characters created by Kenneth Lonergan and Peter Tolan.

July 1998 Draft

A comedy about a psychiatrist whose number one-patient is an insecure mob boss.
